★ This Month in AI & Schools

Federal money is chasing AI, and so is the cheating debate. Here is what that means for us.

Summer reading, AI edition. This month the U.S. Department of Education signaled it wants grant dollars to flow toward AI, which is worth watching as a leadership and budget question. At the same time, the honest conversation about AI and cheating is getting louder, with some schools literally bringing back blue books. We also pulled a few practical pieces on differentiated learning, lesson planning, and teacher burnout so the news is not all hand wringing.
